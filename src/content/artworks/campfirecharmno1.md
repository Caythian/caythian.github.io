description:
  - "\"Campfire Charm\" is an ongoing painting series that examines humanity under the coat of UFO stories. Instead of questioning the truthfulness of UFO encounters, this series focuses on the multi-dimensional impact of the experience itself: the emotional, perceptual, and cognitive. Each painting centers on a narrative about the human subject astounded by a UFO, their perception overwhelmed, and their worldview unsettled. Some pieces draw a parallel between human travel and cosmic travel — placing our cars and planes alongside imagined alien vehicles to scale the human against the cosmic. Others stay closer to the experiencer's perspective, to the moment of seeing. The series isn't really about whether UFOs are real. It's about what the mind does when it tries to hold on to something that won't fully resolve."
